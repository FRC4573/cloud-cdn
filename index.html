About Us

Welcome to the South River Robotics Team website! Our team was created in 2013! you can find our stats from that year on for every competition by CLICKING HERE

That link will give you everything from a detailed match description to a video of the match itself. It’s a great resource to use for scouting as well!

Team 4573 is a close-knit team many would call a family. Our values of teamwork and gracious professionalism carry throughout build season and competitions!

Our Mission Statement

It is our goal as Team 4573 SRNJ Rambotics, to make a comfortable environment that will inspire students to grow, prosper, and stay hungry to learn more. We can achieve our goal by having hands on experience with Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, and Mathematics, or STEAM and with the help of our mentors who have real life experience in those fields.
